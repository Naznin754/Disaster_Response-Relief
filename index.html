User Roles

Tailored experiences for every participant in the relief system

Admin

Central command center for managing all disaster relief operations.

  • Manage all disasters and emergencies
  • Monitor inventory and resources
  • Oversee distribution network
  • Access analytics & reports

Donor

Contribute to relief efforts and track your charitable impact.

  • Make monetary donations
  • Track donation history
  • View impact reports
  • Get tax deduction info

NGO

Manage organizational relief efforts and volunteer coordination.

  • Manage donations
  • Coordinate volunteers
  • Track projects & impact
  • Generate impact reports

Victim

Access relief assistance and track support received.

  • Register for assistance
  • Check relief status
  • View assigned shelter
  • Get distribution updates

Volunteer

Participate in relief activities and assist affected communities.

  • View task assignments
  • Track victim assistance
  • Monitor inventory
  • Report completion

About ReliefHub

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

ReliefHub is dedicated to streamlining disaster response and relief distribution through technology and community collaboration. We connect donors, NGOs, volunteers, and affected communities on a single unified platform to ensure efficient, transparent, and impactful relief operations.

Our Vision

To create a world where disaster relief is coordinated, efficient, and reaches those in need faster. By leveraging technology and community engagement, we aim to minimize suffering, optimize resource utilization, and accelerate recovery in times of crisis.
